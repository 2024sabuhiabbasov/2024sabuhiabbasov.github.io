About Me

I'm Sabuhi, a high school student at UWC ISAK Japan. My passion for tech started with computer science, where I discovered the limitless possibilities of creating anything I could imagine. I've honed my skills in web design, learning WIX during my two-year stint at Kekalove Adoptive Fashion. I've also participated in hackathons and developed skills in HTML, CSS, and Python. In addition, I've been doing competitive programming for about two years, which has allowed me to become skilled in C and C++. I'm pursuing Computer Science as part of my IB program and apply my skills in real-world projects. In my free time, I stay up to date with the latest tech advancements and try new programming languages and frameworks. I'm excited to share my tech experiences and inspire others through this blog.
